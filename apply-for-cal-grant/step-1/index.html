Apply for a Cal Grant: Step 1

Deadline to apply: Monday, March 2, 2020, 11:59 p.m.

Apply for financial aid, if you haven’t already

There are two ways to apply for financial aid that can lead to a Cal Grant. Submit a FAFSA or CADAA form, if you haven’t already.

    Apply for the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) if you:

  • Have U.S. Citizenship
  • Or are a Permanent Resident
  • Or do not have U.S. Citizenship, but you are eligible for federal student aid
  • Or have a T Visa
Apply for FAFSA

    Apply for the California Dream Act Application (CADAA) if you:

  • Are undocumented
  • Or have Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals (DACA) — valid or expired
  • Or have a U Visa
  • Or have Temporary Protected Status (TPS)
  • Or are eligible for AB540
Apply for CADAA
