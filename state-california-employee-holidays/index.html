Weekend holidays

  • When a holiday is on a Saturday, most employees get one holiday credit (eight hours of leave).
  • When a holiday is on a Sunday, most employees have a paid holiday on the following Monday.

Holiday credits

  • Spend your holiday credit on the day or hours you choose.

Personal holidays

  • Most employees get one personal holiday every fiscal year (July 1 to June 30).
  • Take your personal holiday on the day you choose.
  • Most employees start collecting personal holidays after they’ve worked with the State for six months.
