California Office of Digital Innovation

Government services, programs, rules, and operations too often serve the process not the people. That’s why Governor Gavin Newsom created the Office of Digital Innovation (ODI). We use people-centered design and technology to reimagine and deliver equitable, effective services to Caliornians. Better serving people starts with understanding their needs, challenges and expectations.  (Find out how you can join our growing team!)

What we do

We focus on creating and delivering lasting change, not just quick fixes. We work with state agencies and departments to reimagine the core processes, technology systems, and policies that problems are rooted in.

  • Create solutions: Build intuitive digital solutions like websites, data systems, and APIs, that make it easier to access state services.
  • Increase accessibility: Redesign how the state shows information, so all Californians can use it, understand it and get the resources they need.
  • Prototype services: Envision solutions that anticipate Californians’ needs and help them get assistance quickly and with minimal effort.
  • Raise empathy: Gather and share data and insights about Californians’ needs and challenges to improve programs and policy.
  • Improve equity: Identify and eliminate inequity, exclusion, and bias in state program requirements, policies, and technical systems.
  • Build capacity:Equip state workers with the training and tools they need to understand people’s needs and create better services.
  • Drive efficiencies: Streamline state processes by eliminating redundancy, automating steps and reducing paper-to-digital transfer.
  • Frame priorities: Focus leaders’ attention on those issues and problems that matter most to Californians and recommend solutions.
  • Safeguard rights: Encourage data portability, collaboration, and transparency while protecting Californians’ security and privacy.

Who we are

We hire and develop cross-discipline staff with blended backgrounds in tech, design, data, policy, strategy, and research. Our team comes from within the public and private sector. We understand the intricacies of government services and programs. We bring empathy, curiosity, data, technology, and a host of other tools to identifying and solving problems.
